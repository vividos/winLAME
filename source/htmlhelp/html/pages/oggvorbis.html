Ogg Vorbis Settings Page

This is the settings page for generating Ogg Vorbis audio files.

Quality Management

Quality Settings

Lets you choose a quality factor from 0.0 (bad quality) to 10.0 (excellent quality) for the encoding process. A value of 4.0 yields good results. The estimated approximate bitrate is shown to the right.

Bitrate Settings

Warning

These modes are generally not recommended to use for normal encoding! Please use the quality management mode.

Variable Bitrate

Lets you specify a minimum and maximum bitrate the encoder takes as base for encoding.

Average Bitrate

Lets you specify a nominal bitrate. Target bitrate will always be approximately at the nominal bitrate.

Constant Bitrate

Lets you specify a constant bitrate that will be ensured in the whole stream.
